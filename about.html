Hello, world!

My name is Derek Plautz and I am a software engineer. I will be finishing up my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at The Ohio State University in Spring 2017 and continuing my studies by pursuing a Master of Science in CSE at Ohio State as well. I am on track to graduate with my MS in Spring 2018.

I currently have my hands in a diverse set of interests including network programming, computational theory, and front end web development. I love solving complicated problems and writing usable software.

I have experience writing in a multitude of different programming languages and environments. I have worked for a Fortune 15 company writing production code as well as alongside esteemed professors at OSU.

In addition to my work, I have a passion for teaching and academia. I am currently a fourth year undergraduate teaching assistant for the College of Engineering at OSU. I assist classes that introduce programming to incoming freshmen and transfer engineering students.

If you'd like to talk, visit the contact page or email me directly here!
